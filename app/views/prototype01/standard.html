Minimum standard of accessibility

The level of accessibility required for all public sector websites.

All public sector websites must meet a minimum standard of accessibility, which is Level AA of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0. This includes GOV.UK and all services that appear on it.

All services must be tested with disabled and older people throughout the different phases of design.
