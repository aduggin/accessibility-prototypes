Minimum standard of accessibility

The level of accessibility required for all content published on GOV.UK

Technical accessibility

The minimum standard of accessibility for all public sector websites is Level AA of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.0.

This applies to all web pages on GOV.UK and all web pages created for government services.

Usable accessibility

All services must be tested with disabled and older people throughout the different phases of design.
