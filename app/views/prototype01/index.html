Minimum standard of accessibility

Legal context

There is a legal requirement to not discrimate against people with disabilities

Benefits of making services accessible

In addition to the legal drivers, there are social, financial and technical incentives for accessibility.

Measuring accessibility

The only way to find out if a website is accessible is to test it.

Impairments to consider

There are four main categories of impairment to consider. These are vision impairment; motor difficulties; cognitive and learning; and deaf and hard of hearing.

Assistive Technology

Assistive technologies are any item, piece of equipment, software or hardware system that helps a person with disabilities to interact with computers.
